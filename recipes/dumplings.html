Dumplings Recipe

Description

A sure-fire way to bring everyone at your table together? Chicken and dumplings!Chicken and dumplings is a soup-like dish that consists of chicken, a thick broth, and dumplings made out of biscuit dough. The iconic comfort food is filling, made with cheap ingredients, and deliciously cozy.

Ingredients

  • Chicken-Opt For Boneless, Skin Chicken Breasts
  • Butter
  • Canned Soup-Two Cans Of Condensed Cream Of Chicken Soup/li>
  • An Onion
  • Refrigerated Biscuit Dough

Steps

One of the easiest recipes you'll ever encounter. It couldn't be easier to make Crock-Pot chicken and dumplings.

  1. Add the chicken, butter, soup, and onion to a slow cooker and cook on high for about five or six hours.
  2. Add the shredded biscuit dough about 30 minutes before serving. Let the dough cook in the soup until it's no longer raw.
  3. You may also add a little flavor with spices and seasonings they have on hand, such as paprika and garlic. This recipe is easily customizable, so spice it up however you please.
